# App Privacy Questions - Answers for Lingoa

## Initial Question

**"Do you or your third-party partners collect data from this app?"**

✅ **Answer: Yes, we collect data from this app**

---

## Data Types to Select

After selecting "Yes", you'll be asked about specific data types. Select these:

### ✅ Audio Data
- **Purpose**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: No
- **Used for Tracking**: No
- **Details**: Microphone recordings used for speech recognition and language practice

### ✅ User Content
- **Purpose**: App Functionality  
- **Linked to User**: No
- **Used for Tracking**: No
- **Details**: Voice recordings and transcribed text used for conversation practice

---

## Third-Party Partners

You'll need to disclose third-party partners:

### OpenAI
- **Services Used**: 
  - Whisper API (speech-to-text)
  - GPT-4o (AI conversation)
  - Text-to-Speech API
- **Data Shared**: Audio recordings, transcribed text, conversation transcripts
- **Purpose**: App Functionality (language learning)
- **Linked to User**: No
- **Used for Tracking**: No

### ElevenLabs (if configured)
- **Services Used**: Text-to-Speech
- **Data Shared**: Text content for speech generation
- **Purpose**: App Functionality
- **Linked to User**: No
- **Used for Tracking**: No

---

## Important Notes

- **"Collect"** means transmitting data off the device
- Your app sends audio/text to OpenAI for processing
- Data is processed in real-time and not stored permanently
- No user accounts = data not linked to user identity
- No advertising/tracking = not used for tracking
